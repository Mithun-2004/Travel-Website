Ooty Nature Odyssey

Duration: 5 Days / 4 Nights

Price: $799 per person

Description

Escape to the charming hill town of Ooty, located in the Nilgiri Hills of Southern India, with our 5-day Ooty Nature Odyssey. This package is tailor-made for nature enthusiasts and those seeking a tranquil getaway. Immerse yourself in the verdant landscapes, picturesque gardens, and colonial architecture that define this idyllic destination. From serene boat rides on the Ooty Lake to exploring the lush Botanical Gardens, this package promises an unforgettable blend of relaxation and exploration in the lap of nature.

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation in a heritage hotel with modern amenities
  • Scenic ride on the Nilgiri Mountain Railway (Toy Train)
  • Visit to the sprawling Rose Garden, a visual delight
  • Exploration of the historic Ooty Government Botanical Gardens
  • Guided tour of a local tea estate with a tea-tasting experience
  • Boat ride on the serene Ooty Lake with picturesque views
  • Daily breakfast featuring both continental and regional dishes
  • Round-trip transportation from the nearest airport

Embrace the tranquility of Ooty's hills and let the natural beauty rejuvenate your soul. Reserve your spot now for an enchanting Ooty Nature Odyssey!
