Darjeeling Himalayan Discovery

Duration: 5 Days / 4 Nights

Price: $699 per person

Description

Embark on a captivating journey to the enchanting hill station of Darjeeling with our 5-day Darjeeling Himalayan Discovery package. Nestled amidst the Eastern Himalayas, Darjeeling is renowned for its panoramic vistas, tea plantations, and unique cultural charm. This package offers a blend of breathtaking landscapes, colonial heritage, and warm local hospitality, ensuring an unforgettable experience in the lap of the Himalayas.

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation in a charming heritage hotel with mountain views
  • Sunrise visit to Tiger Hill for panoramic views of the Himalayas
  • Guided tour of tea estates and insight into tea processing
  • Exploration of the vibrant local markets and handicrafts
  • Ride on the iconic Darjeeling Himalayan Railway (Toy Train)
  • Visit to the Himalayan Mountaineering Institute and Zoo
  • Daily breakfast featuring local and continental delicacies
  • Round-trip transportation from the nearest airport

Join us on the Darjeeling Himalayan Discovery to discover the serene beauty and cultural richness of this hill station. Reserve your spot now for an unforgettable journey!
