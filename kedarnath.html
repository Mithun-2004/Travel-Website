Journey to Kedarnath

Duration: 6 Days / 5 Nights

Price: $799 per person

Description

Embark on a sacred journey to Kedarnath, one of the holiest pilgrimage sites in the Himalayas, with our 6-day Journey to Kedarnath package. This pilgrimage offers a deeply spiritual experience as you traverse through picturesque landscapes and immerse yourself in the divine atmosphere of the Himalayas. Witness the grandeur of the Kedarnath Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, and pay your respects at this revered shrine, surrounded by snow-capped peaks.

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation in comfortable lodges along the pilgrimage route
  • Guided trek to Kedarnath Temple, with mule and porter services
  • Spiritual sessions and group prayers led by experienced guides
  • Visit to the Adi Shankaracharya Samadhi, a place of significance
  • Opportunity for a refreshing dip in the Tapt Kund hot springs
  • Daily vegetarian meals in accordance with pilgrimage traditions
  • Round-trip transportation from the nearest airport
